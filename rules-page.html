General Rules

  • Be respectful to all users
  • No spam or excessive posting
  • No inappropriate content or NSFW material
  • Keep discussions on-topic within their respective threads
  • No advertising or self-promotion without permission

Posting Guidelines

  • Check if a similar thread exists before creating a new one
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for new threads
  • Mark spoilers appropriately
  • Credit artists when sharing artwork
  • No cross-posting the same content in multiple threads

Content Rules

  • No pirated content or illegal streaming links
  • No hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Keep criticism constructive
  • No political discussions outside designated threads
  • Respect copyright and fair use guidelines

Consequences

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: 24-hour posting restriction
  • Third offense: 1-week suspension
  • Severe violations may result in immediate permanent ban
  • Ban appeals can be made through the contact form
