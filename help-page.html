Getting Started

How do I create a new thread?
Go to the relevant board and use the "New Thread" form at the top. Enter a title and your message, then click "Create Thread".
How do I reply to a thread?
Scroll to the bottom of the thread and use the reply form. You can quote other posts using >>post-number.

Text Formatting

How do I quote other posts?
Use >>post-number to quote specific posts. Example:
>>1 Great point!
How do I mark spoilers?
Use [spoiler] tags around your text:
[spoiler]Plot twist details here[/spoiler]

Account Management

How do I change my password?
Go to your profile settings and click on "Change Password". Follow the prompts to update your password.
I forgot my password, what should I do?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via email.

Common Issues

Why can't I create a new thread?
New accounts must wait 24 hours before creating threads. This helps prevent spam.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the "Report" button below any post to alert moderators about rule violations.
